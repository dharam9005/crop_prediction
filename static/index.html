About FarmAI

FarmAI is an AI-powered crop production prediction platform built to help farmers, agri-businesses, and policy makers make data-driven decisions.

Our machine learning model is trained on 18+ years of real Indian agricultural data covering 33 states, 700+ districts, and 124 crop varieties. It uses a Random Forest algorithm to predict crop production based on location, crop type, season, year, and cultivated area.

Built by Dharmender Singh Yadav, a B.Tech graduate in AI & Data Science with deep roots in farming — because technology should serve the people who feed the nation.
